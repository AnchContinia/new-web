Frequently asked questions

How can finance automation software improve financial processes in Business Central?
By automating repetitive tasks such as data entry, invoice processing, and document management, finance automation software for Business Central can reduce errors, speed up processing times, and provide better financial insights. Organizations typically experience:
  • Up to 90% reduction in manual data entry
  • Faster invoice processing times
  • Improved accuracy with automated data capture
  • Enhanced compliance and audit trails
  • Significant cost savings in accounts payable operations
  • Greater employee satisfaction
Why choose Continia for finance automation in Business Central?
Continia offers specialized finance automation tools built exclusively for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. Our solutions, such as Document Capture, Expense Management, and Continia Banking, are built directly within Business Central, ensuring a smooth, efficient, and user-friendly experience. This built-in approach simplifies installation, reduces maintenance efforts, and allows immediate use without the need to learn new interfaces.
Can finance automation software for Business Central help with compliance and audit trails?
Yes, Continia’s finance automation tools ensure that all transactions, approvals, and document flows are recorded and easily accessible, providing an audit trail that supports regulatory compliance and internal audits. Both Expense Management and Document Capture use a secure digital archive that protects your original bookkeeping files against tampering or accidental deletions.
What financial processes can be automated in Business Central?
Key processes that can be automated include:
  • Accounts Payable (AP) invoice processing
  • Purchase order matching
  • Payment processing and reconciliation
  • Expense management and reporting
  • Document capture and data extraction
  • Approval workflows
  • Bank reconciliation
By integrating Continia's solutions, businesses can further streamline their financial operations, leading to significant time and cost savings.
How much does Continia’s finance automation software for Business Central cost?
The pricing varies based on factors such as the specific product, deployment method (on-premises or cloud), and the number of users or transactions. For Business Central Online, Continia provides a "Use & Pay" licensing model, where you're invoiced based on your usage and activated feature modules at the end of each month. Continia's pricing structure is fully transparent and can be accessed through the pricing tab, where you will find our price calculator and price sheets for each of our solutions.
How easy is it to integrate Continia solutions with Business Central?
Integrating Continia solutions with Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is incredibly straightforward. Designed as plug-and-play add-ons, Continia solutions require minimal setup time and no additional third-party software. Once installed, they integrate directly into your Business Central environment, ensuring smooth operation with existing workflows.

Continia solutions are compatible with both cloud and on-premises versions of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. They support the latest Business Central versions and maintain backward compatibility with earlier updates. Continia is also available for Microsoft Dynamics NAV. On Continia Docs, you can get an overview of the supported versions for each Continia solution.
